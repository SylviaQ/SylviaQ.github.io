What to Expect When You Are Expecting


There are a lot of surprises that come with parenthood, but cost of childbirth delivery should not be one of them. We were seeking a way to help expectant parents get mentally prepared for childbirth delivery cost with less efforts. After the process of user research, sketching, prototyping and evaluation, we came up with a solution called E$PECT.
